Baby Yoda

Did you know that Baby Yoda, also known as Grogu, is a character from the Star Wars universe? He first appeared in the Disney+ series "The Mandalorian".

He is of the same species as the famous Jedi Master Yoda, but his origins and his connection to the Force remain mysterious.

"Cute, he is." - Master Yoda

Fun Fact: Baby Yoda's real name is Grogu!
